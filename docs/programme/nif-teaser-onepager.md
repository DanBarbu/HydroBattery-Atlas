## In one sentence

WILL is an open-core, sovereign, multi-tenant Command and Control fabric for NATO's Eastern Flank that absorbs any sensor or effector via a typed plugin SDK in under four hours.

## The problem

NATO members on the Eastern Flank — and Romania most prominently — are buying tactical platforms (F-35, Patriot, HIMARS, Bayraktar, naval combatants, USVs) faster than they can integrate them into a coherent common operating picture. Every new system arrives with its own C2 silo. The integration cost is paid in operator workload, in lost minutes during contact, and in foreign-vendor dependency.

## The solution

A Lattice-class platform, sovereign by construction, white-label by design:

- **Plugin SDK** (gRPC, contract-versioned) — any sensor in under four hours; demonstrated against ATAK-MIL CoT today, MAVLink autopilots in Sprint 2, STANAG 4607 GMTI in Sprint 3, LoRa IoT in Sprint 4.
- **Multi-tenant white-label** — defence integrators OEM the platform under their own brand; isolated tenants per branch of forces or coalition member.
- **Three deployment profiles, one Helm chart** — EU Sovereign Cloud, Romanian Government Private Cloud (CPG/STS), on-premises air-gapped. NATO RESTRICTED through (Sprint 11) NATO SECRET-ready.
- **NATO-interoperable from the spine** — STANAG 4774 confidentiality labels structural since Sprint 0; Link-16 / Link-22 / MIP 4 / FMN Spiral 4 conformance on the roadmap.

## Why now

- **SAFE** (EU Reg. 2025/1142) opens a EUR 150 B loan envelope for joint defence procurement in the EU; WILL's plugin Bill of Origin process (Sprint 12) is the eligibility hook.
- **DIANA** opens Romania-hosted test centres and annual cohort calls in priority areas where WILL competes (autonomy, secure communications, sensing).
- **Romanian MoD modernisation** is the largest single defence acquisition wave in the country's modern history; the integrator that owns the C2 fabric layer earns recurring revenue across every programme.

## What we are not

- We are not an exquisite system; we are a fabric. Exquisite systems plug in.
- We are not US- or NATO-vendor dependent at the runtime layer; the core is in-house Romanian.
- We are not closed-source at the spine; we are open-core. The crown jewels are the SDK contract and the multi-tenant runtime.
